Rules for Examinations

  1. Adoption of e-Governance (online submission of exam application, online payment of exam fee and instant generation / printing of e-Hall ticket) to operate with Paperless Communication and Cashless Transactions effectively.
  2. Communication with Departments / Colleges / Institutes through dedicated institutional e-mails.
  3. Pre-Exam Process (including customized Barcoded Answer Booklets)
  4. On-Exam Process (Computer coded Attendance Sheets)
  5. Post-Exam Process (scanning and generation of marks)
  6. Publication of Results and Post-Result Process (Re-valuation etc)
  7. Issue of Certificates
  8. Report Generation and Preservation of Data

The details of the examination centers can be known through the circulars passed in schools.It Students are also advised to visit the website of their schools for Examination related information. Students may contact the respective wings for examination related information.
